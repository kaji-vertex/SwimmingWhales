The thing about Swimming... and STROKES!

Swimming offers tremendous fitness benefits. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention says that just 2.5 hours of swimming per week decreases the risk of chronic illnesses. Swimming improves mood and decreases anxiety, and the buoyancy of the water makes swimming a good choice for those with arthritis and other pain conditions. Even if you have no plans to become the next Michael Phelps, basic swimming skills are easy to learn.
